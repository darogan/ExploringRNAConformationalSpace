Challenge 12: ydaO riboswitch
This problem consists in predicting the structure of a RNA molecule with the following sequence:

5'-AUCGCUGAACGCGGGGGACCCAGGGGGCGAAUCUCUUCCGAAAGGAAGAGU
   AGGGUUACUCCUUCGACCCGAGCCCGUCAGCUAACCUCGCAAGCGUCCGAA
   GGAGAAAAA-3'
